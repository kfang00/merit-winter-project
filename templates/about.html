This is a Clothing Website developed by Choi Ying Yau, Sylvia Qu and Kayla Fang. Here is the following detail about us:

UNreality aims to be an ECO-friendly store, and our current sustainability goal is that all of the clothings from our branding will only be made of cotton, linen and polyester that's organic or recycled.

As mentioned, the cloths that we offer is made of sustainable cotton, linen and polyester, and our mission is to provide clothings that will be comfortable for customers to wear of. For all of the materials, they can be safely dry-cleaned or machine-washed(cold or warm water). But remember to restrict your washing to similar colors and avoid anything that has deep or intense dyes so that the clothes with stay with its original color.
