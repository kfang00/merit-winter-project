Site Policy

Privacy Policy

We respect and value our customer's privacy and therefore handle personal information carefully. We will not share the personal information that we obtained from the customers to others. We only use part of the information for marketing promotional use: such as email or text message for new arrivals / discount coupon for holidays / anniversary. By using this site, you have consented our use of email as a mean to notification. Other than that, we also collect information of your purchase history and purchase date.

Return Policy

Our return policy lasts 14 days. Unfortunately, if 14 days have gone by the day you receives items, we are unable to offer you a refund. To complete your return, we require a e-receipt or proof of purchase. Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email for notification. We will also notify you of our approval or rejection of your refund. If it is approved, your refund will be issued back to the original form of payment. If the items are defective, we can provide exchange or refund based on your choice.
* Notice: Only regular priced items may be refunded, unfortunately sale items cannot be refunded. Also, the shipping costs of your return are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

Shipping Policy

Our store usually ships out items within 48 hours from the date received your order. You will be notified by email if there is any extraordinary circumstance.
* Notice: Shipping delays may happen due to COVID 19 / weather events.
